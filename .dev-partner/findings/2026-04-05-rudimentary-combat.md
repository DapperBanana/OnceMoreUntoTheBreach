The introduction of a basic combat system is crucial for this type of game. Now is the time to consider how to expand upon this foundation. Things to explore are adding more stats (attack, defense, speed), varying enemy types with different behaviors, and incorporating some form of randomness (dice rolls, critical hits) to make combat more engaging and less predictable. Also think about adding a way for the player to avoid combat in some scenarios.
